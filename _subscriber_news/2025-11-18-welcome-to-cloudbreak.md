## What Makes CloudBreak Different?

**Privacy First**: We don't track what you read, we don't analyze your feeds, and we don't sell your data. Your RSS subscriptions and reading history are yours alone.

**Open Source Foundation**: CloudBreak is built on trusted open-source software. No black boxes, no vendor lock-in, just transparent technology you can trust.

**Your Data, Your Control**: Export your feeds anytime via OPML. We want you to stay because you love the service, not because you are locked in.

## Getting Started

Setting up your RSS reader is simple:

1. **Import Your Feeds**: Have existing subscriptions? Import them using OPML format from your previous reader.  In FreshRSS, go to the **Subscription Management** section to import an OPML file from your previous reader.
2. **Discover New Content**: Browse the web and subscribe to your favorite blogs and news sites directly.
3. **Read on Your Terms**: No algorithms deciding what you should see. Just your feeds, in chronological order, the way news was meant to be.
