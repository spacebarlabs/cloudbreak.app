About

Privacy-focused apps, made easy

Our Mission

Deliver the uncompromising privacy of self-hosting with the frictionless convenience of the cloud.

We democratize access to open source software. We make it easy for anyone to run privacy-respecting tools without the complexity of managing servers themselves.

What We Do

CloudBreak is a product of Space Bar Labs, an LLC based in Iowa City, IA.

We host open source software as a service, so you get the privacy benefits of self-hosting without the hassle. See our full product list.

Who Are We

Benjamin Oakes

Founder, Space Bar Labs

Ben has been building software professionally since 2006, with a focus on Ruby. He started Space Bar Labs to deliver meaningful software and lean into entrepreneurship. He is an open source enthusiast. Ben is the only full time member of Space Bar Labs.
