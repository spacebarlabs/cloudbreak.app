Frequently Asked Questions

What is CloudBreak?

We provide managed hosting for a curated set of open-source applications, so you can enjoy the benefits of great software without the hassle of running your own server. We handle installation, updates, and maintenance.

Why choose CloudBreak over a free service?

When a service is free, *you* are often the product. Our business model is simple: you pay a small fee for a great service. This allows us to be sustainable while protecting your privacy, not exploiting it.
